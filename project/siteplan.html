Mzanzi Foodie

Site Name

Mzanzi Foodie

I chose this name for the purpose of the name "Mzanzi" because it represents a vibrant, mordern identity for the nation. This name is frequently used in pop culture, fashion, music and media to evoke a sense of pride, community, and "South africanness". As much as I might have wanted to create a name that would be easier for many tourist to pronounce, Mzanzi reflects more on the country. Since this website is a South African Food website, it fits the it perfectly.

Optional domain availability: mzanzi-bar.co.za

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website will be featuring and showcasing profiles of local street vendors or businesses to tourists, popular eats in Mzanzi and a map of where you can go to get specific kinds of foods around the country for the locals and for the people around the world.

Scenarios

  • "What are the food recommendation I can try out in a specific area around SA?"
  • "Where can I find the contact details for a specific resturant or fast food place? "
